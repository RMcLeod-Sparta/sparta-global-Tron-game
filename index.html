Blue Controls: (W=up) (D=right) (S=down) (A=left)

Orange Controls: (I=up) (L=right) (K=down) (J=left)

Red Controls: (T=up) (H=right) (G=down) (F=left)

Purple Controls: ([=up) (\=right) ('=down) (;=left)



Press Any Key to Start and P to Pause

Hit 2, 3 or 4 to choose the number of players

Nort is played by using your 4 directional keys to navigate the grid. You lose by colliding with any trail left by any player as well as the surrounding wall. The winner is the last one left standing.
